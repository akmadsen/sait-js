Quote Machine

Of course, these accomplishments were only possible with copious amounts of help and inspiration from StackOverflow, Codepen, and our amazingly insightful instructors.

So what's the point?

In particular here, I wanted to demonstrate that I could make a functional, animated carousel. The main source of functional inspiration was from this codepen by Lucas Motta.
